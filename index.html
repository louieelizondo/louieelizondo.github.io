About / Sobre mí

Operator first,
developer in progress.

I run Natural Balance — a food business in Chihuahua selling homestyle meals: prepped, cooked, refrigerated, frozen, and hot-ready combos for families who want to eat well without the daily cooking grind.

Instead of just running the business the traditional way, I rebuilt it around technology. Notion runs operations. Shopify runs sales. The result is a lean, modern business that punches above its weight.

Now I'm going further — learning to build the software I always wanted but couldn't find. This portfolio is my public learning log. Every project here started as a real problem.

Soy presidente de la Sección de Restauranteros en CANACO Chihuahua — trabajando para que más negocios locales se modernicen y crezcan.
